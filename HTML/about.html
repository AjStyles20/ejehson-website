Our Journey

2008

Founding of Ejehson & Sons

Established in Birnin Kebbi by Solomon Ejeh with a small shop on Ahmadu Bello Way, focusing on basic electrical components for local contractors.

2012

Expansion into Industrial Supply

Began supplying transformers and heavy cables to Kebbi State's growing industrial sector, establishing partnerships with major manufacturers.

2015

Family Leadership Transition

Ignatius and Francis Ejeh joined the business, bringing fresh perspectives and expanding our product range to include premium Nigerian-made cables.

2020

Infrastructure Growth

Played a key role in Kebbi State's power infrastructure projects, supplying critical components for rural electrification initiatives.

2023

Modernization & Growth

Implemented digital operations while maintaining our commitment to personalized service, serving over 500 clients across Northwest Nigeria.
